Format Recommendations
Why This Order
R1 · Willbrook → 2-Man Scramble
Willbrook is the most forgiving course — wider fairways, beautiful but not punishing. Perfect opener to shake off travel. The scramble format keeps everyone relaxed and social.
R2 · River Club → 4-Man Best Ball
River Club has water on 15 of 18 holes and 100+ bunkers. Best ball is ideal — the team absorbs blowup holes while the hot hand carries the score.
R3 · Pawleys Plantation → Stroke Play
Pawleys is the crown jewel — Jack Nicklaus design, slope 150, marsh par 3s on 13 and 17. The championship round where every stroke matters on the most dramatic course of the trip.
R4 · Founders Club → Fun Round 🍺
No points, no pressure. The trip champion is crowned after Pawleys. Founders is the send-off round — just cold drinks and good stories.

Handicap Stroke Allocation
How It Works
Each player receives strokes on the holes rated equal to or harder than their handicap index. A 12 handicap gets 1 stroke on holes rated 1–12. Gold dots (●) on your scorecard show exactly which holes you receive strokes on — one dot per stroke.
Net score = gross minus strokes received on that hole. The app calculates all net scores automatically.
